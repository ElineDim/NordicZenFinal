Book your cabin


Surround yourself with the magic of the north. Do you see yourself sitting in a comfy wool-covered chair, under a knitted blanket, reading a book with a cup of damping chocolate by your side? Book one of our Zen cabins and experience the magic of the north.





The Zen tent

The Zen tent spreads heat and a cosy atmosphere inside it's tiny space. You will feel overwhelmed with the nordic feeling of hygge as soon as you step inside. Due to its high sides, you can stand straight up at almost every spot.

The space is heated with a wood burner so you don't need to worry about the thin tent walls. The beds are covered in fur-like material and keep heat while giving you a soft layer to comfortably sink deep into. If you do happen to feel cold, you are welcome to use one of our wool knits!


Heating

Use the stove inside the tent to heat the space, or create a bonfire outside and keep the tent open while you enjoy the amazing view.

Sanitary

Fresh water and sanitary rooms are available within 200 meters.

The Zen hut

Our Zen hut offers the perfect combination between a comfortable home and a cosy space close to nature. The big window in the back provides an amazing view over the mountains and you will never miss a glimpse of the dancing Northern lights. The occasional elk might even come and say hi!

There is plenty of wool and knitwear to keep you warm and we have installed a small kitchen so you can make your own hot chocolate. The furniture is transformable to suit everyones needs.


Heating

The hut has a wood burner to provide as much heat as you need.

Sanitary

You will find a fully functioning bathroom in every hut.

The Zen home

You want to enjoy a good dose of luxury while watching the magic colours in front of your window? The Zen home offers you exactly that. This home has everything you need and a little bit more than that.

You can use the kitchen with oven and microwave for easy dinners after a wonderful hike outside in the snow. You will also find a fridge in the kitchen. A freezer box can be found outside, right next to the entrance, where there is a 100% guarantee of frost. And don't worry about food supplies, we will get you covered. A bottle of Glögg is ready for heating!


Heating

The fireplace will provide enough heat to keep the whole house warm. We will happily fill up the wood stock if neccessary.

Sanitary

All Zen homes have a fully functioning bathroom as well as a hot tub outside.
