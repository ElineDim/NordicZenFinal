About Nordic Zen




Nordic Zen is a project that started in 2002 by our amazing team. We wanted to share this magical place with everyone, and came up with the idea of the cabins. Every year we have 1200+ visitors from all around the world.




How it started

We, who invented Nordic Zen, love being in nature! We needed to share it with many others and show how fantastic it can be to enjoy nature, let alone the northern lights. The amazing wildlife in the north, makes your stay in our cabins to an unforgettable experience. There are many options if you book a stay with us, as we have both tours you can take, as well as delicious meals.
