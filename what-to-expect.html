What to expect




Need a break? This is the perfect place then!
Enjoy the quietness in this magical place of the north. We will make sure you have the time of your life. This is the place for both relaxation and amazing experiences.



The Experience

Nordic Zen is a place where you can take a deep breath and blow out all stress, worries and other things and just be in zen. To be fully relaxed and provide "self-care". At Nordic Zen, we have scents that are 100% neutral for your soul. Our decor is Scandinavian and gives you a feeling of a good quality of life

The Surroundings


Discover the beautiful snowy landscape of the north. The place that is known for its northern lights, is truely a magical place. If your bringing your patience, you might even be lucky enough, to discover the wildlife of the north.



Activities

Is the surroundings not enough for you?
Don't worry. We have a bunch of activities!


Bonfire

We have all the neccessary things to make your own bonfire (including the marshmellows!). The firewood will be restocked everyday.


Snow Scooter

We have guided tours on snow scooters. This is a great way to discover the magical landscape! Contact the staff to book a guided tour. It's included in the price!


Ice Fishing

Enjoy to fish? We have fishing rods available, if you want to catch your own dinner. We also have the tools to cut a hole in the ice.
Contact the staff to hear more.
